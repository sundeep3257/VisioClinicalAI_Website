About us

VisioClinical AI is a clinical AI software company focused on making remote physical examination more objective, accessible, and scalable.

The company was built around a simple clinical insight: virtual care cannot reach its full potential if clinicians can only hear what patients say, but cannot reliably measure what patients show.

Our team is developing camera-based tools that help providers capture meaningful physical exam data from standard patient devices. Beginning with ArmorMed for automated range of motion and mobility assessment, VisioClinical AI is expanding into additional visual assessment domains including wound monitoring and neurologic screening.

Our goal is to help care teams deliver more informed, efficient, and accessible care without requiring patients to travel for every routine physical assessment.
